Hannes F. Wagner

Full Professor of Finance · Bocconi University

I am a Full Professor of Finance, a Fellow at the IGIER Institute for Economic Research, and an appointed Member of the ECGI European Corporate Governance Institute. I am currently Visiting Faculty at NUS Business School.

My research focuses on institutional investors, shareholder engagement, corporate governance, and corporate social responsibility. My work has appeared in the Journal of Finance, Review of Financial Studies, Journal of Financial Economics, and Review of Finance, and has been featured in The Economist, the Financial Times, Bloomberg, Reuters, and the Harvard Business Review.

I received my PhD in Finance from Ludwig Maximilians University Munich in 2005.
